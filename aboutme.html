Who am I?

My name is Tamaine Ottley, and I am a junior in high school in Belize. I have a love for technology, and this is one of the first websites I have made. I chose to join SOC Cowders because I am always eager to learn new things and develop my skills.

Why did I choose food industrialization as my topic?

I chose food industrialization after watching a documentary about it on Youtube. At the end of the day, we are what we eat, and I wanted to bring awareness to this. If we take eating healthy seriously it will have a huge impact on public health. Furthermore, It will result in a society working at optimal capacity. We must choose the right fuel for our bodies.
